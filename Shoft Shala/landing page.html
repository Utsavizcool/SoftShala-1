Share Hub Nepal


Share Hub Nepal is a comprehensive mobile application developed by Softshala Nepal, designed to assist investors in navigating the Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE). Available on both Android and iOS platforms, the app offers a suite of features tailored for both novice and seasoned investors.

Key Features

Real-Time Market Data

Access live updates on stock prices, trading volumes, and market indices.

Alerts and Notifications

Receive timely alerts on IPOs, FPOs, dividends, and other significant market events

Portfolio Management

Create and manage multiple portfolios to track your investments effectively.

Advanced Analysis Tools

Utilize fundamental and technical analysis tools, including advanced charting and historical data..

Educational Resources

Enhance your understanding of the stock market with educational content and insights.

Additional Financial Information

Stay informed with updates on forex rates, gold/silver prices, and global market indices.
